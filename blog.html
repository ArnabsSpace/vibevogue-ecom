Sustainably Sourced Materials

At VibeVogue, we believe in creating with purpose—investing in the now to design a better, more sustainable future. That’s why we’re committed to sourcing high-quality materials that are gentler on the planet.

Cotton
Admin  ·  September 2024

At VibeVogue, we source certified organic cotton—grown without harmful pesticides or fertilizers, and using significantly less water than conventional cotton.

By avoiding toxic pesticides, we also preserve biodiversity and protect the health and well-being of farming communities.
