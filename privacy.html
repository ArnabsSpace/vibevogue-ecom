Privacy Policy

At VibeVogue, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email address, shipping address, and phone number.
  • Payment information when you make a purchase.
  • Browsing data through cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To personalize your shopping experience.
  • To send order updates, promotional offers, and newsletters (only if you opt in).
  • To improve our website performance and customer service.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g., shipping partners, payment gateways) solely to complete your order or improve our services.

4. Data Security

Your information is securely stored and protected using industry-standard encryption and firewall technology. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, track preferences, and collect analytics data. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with a revised effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@vibevogue.com.
